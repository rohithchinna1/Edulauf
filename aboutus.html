>E-learning refers to the use of electronic media and information and communication technologies in education.

>E-learning is broadly inclusive of all forms of educational technology in learning and teaching.

>E-learning is inclusive of, and is broadly synonymous with multimedia learning, technology-enhanced learning, computer-based instruction, computer-based training, computer-assisted instruction or computer-aided instruction, internet-based training, web-based training, online education, virtual education, virtual learning environments which are also called learning platforms, m-learning, and digital educational collaboration.
